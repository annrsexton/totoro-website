My Neighbor Totoro

About Totoro

My Neighbor Totoro is a 1988 Japanese animated fantasy film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki and animated by Studio Ghibli.

My Neighbor Totoro: Original movie poster.

More info about Totoro

  1. My Neighbor Totoro came out in 1988 but was dubbed for the first time in 1989
  2. The main characters are Satsuki, Mei, Tatsuo, and Yasuko. We also can't forget Totoro himself of course.
